Teacher Guide

Free, classroom‑ready ideas for grades K–4: reading comprehension, SEL, cross‑curricular projects, assessments, and printables.

Quick Start

One‑Day Read Aloud

  • Picture walk + prediction chart
  • Think‑aloud on confidence & kindness
  • Exit Ticket: “Today I noticed…”

Three‑Day Mini Unit

  • Day 1: Characters & setting
  • Day 2: Sequencing the journey
  • Day 3: Theme + quick write

Two‑Week Plan

  • SEL lessons (Confidence → Wisdom)
  • Map skills & art integration
  • Narrative writing + gallery walk

Learning Objectives

ELA

  • Ask/answer questions; recount key details
  • Determine theme; cite text‑based evidence
  • Narrative writing with structure & voice

SEL

  • Recognize & manage emotions
  • Practice kindness & problem‑solving
  • Build confidence, courage, determination

Cross‑Curricular

  • Map reading & legend/scale
  • Light & shadow exploration
  • Math: shapes, symmetry, graphing

Lesson Ideas by Grade

K–1

  • Picture sequencing cards
  • Feelings & faces (emoji match)
  • Centers: art, dramatic play, word work

2–3

  • Trait & evidence T‑charts
  • Cause & effect chains
  • Narrative: My own treasure map

4

  • Symbolism of flowers & candles
  • POV rewrite (horse’s perspective)
  • Opinion: Which value matters most?
