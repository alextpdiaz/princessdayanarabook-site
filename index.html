About the Book

In this heartwarming children’s adventure, Princess Dayanara discovers a mysterious map marked “Priceless Treasure” and embarks on a journey across the Kingdom of Sagemont. Guided by magical flowers and candles representing confidence, kindness, determination, courage, and wisdom, she faces challenges, overcomes fears, and learns timeless life lessons that resonate with readers of all ages.

Editorial Reviews

"A magical and uplifting children's adventure that teaches the greatest treasure is the one we carry within."

In this beautifully written and richly imaginative tale, young readers follow Princess Dayanara as she sets out across the Kingdom of Sagemont in search of a priceless treasure. Guided by a mysterious map, she encounters challenges, overcomes fears, and learns powerful life lessons about courage, kindness, and self-belief.

With every turn of the page, children discover that bravery is born from facing fears, kindness can bridge any gap, and wisdom often comes from the journey itself. The vivid storytelling and enchanting imagery make this a perfect bedtime read, classroom discussion starter, or gift for any child who loves princess adventures with heart.

About the Author

Alexander Diaz is a passionate storyteller dedicated to inspiring young minds through uplifting tales of courage, kindness, and perseverance. With a deep appreciation for the magic of childhood imagination, he crafts stories that not only entertain but also teach important life lessons. Princess Dayanara and the Priceless Treasure is his debut children’s book, written with love to spark hope, confidence, and curiosity in readers everywhere.
